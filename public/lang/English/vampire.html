Vampire

Quick Overview

Vampire origin based on the vampire from the minecraft mod witchery.

Strengths:

  • Food bar is replaced with blood bar
  • Can suck blood out of livig being dealing damage
  • Can use blood to regenerate hp
  • Can use blood to hide from the sun
  • You are immortal as long as you have blood

Weaknesses:

  • Can't sleep
  • Can't regenerate hp naturaly
  • Burn to sun light
  • Fire deals twice the damage
  • Feel nauseous when low on blood

Powers

Blood Bar

Blood Bar is a simple power that is meant to replace the food bar a player normaly have. When the bar is over 90% full you gain resistance and strengh and when it is below 10% gain weaknes and nosea. Apart from that, the blood bar does nothing much more then store the blood that can be gain amd used other powers.
