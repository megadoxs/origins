Werewolf

Quick Overview

Werewolf origin based on Warkick from league of legends.

Strengths:

  • You transform into a beast at night and an even scarier one on full moons
  • Smelling blood enrages you and can only be calmed down by shedding some more
  • You can smell hurt enemy that are loosing blood from afar
  • When you are in your best form, driking the blood of your enemy heals you

Weaknesses:

  • You might want to stay away from iron
  • You can only eat meat
  • When you de-transform your body weakens
  • You have lost the ability to heal naturaly
